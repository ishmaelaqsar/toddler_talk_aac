* Toddler Talk - AAC for Early Intervention

Toddler Talk is an Android (and iOS) application designed specifically for non-verbal toddlers (ages 1-3).
Unlike general AAC apps, it prioritizes **instant feedback**, **large motor targets**, and **safety** to prevent accidental edits by curious fingers.
It utilizes evidence-based strategies for early intervention, focusing on specific "Needs" over abstract "Core Words."

3. Run on device:

** ðŸ“– Parent Guide

*** How to Edit Cards
1. Open the app.
2. Find the **Lock Icon** in the top right corner.
3. **Press and Hold** (Long Press) for 1 second until you see "Mom Mode".
4. You will now see:
  - A "+" button to add new cards.
  - A "Menu" (three dots) button for backups.
  - "Delete" (X) icons on custom cards.
  - You can now **Drag and Drop** cards to reorder them.

*** How to Add a Custom Card
1. Tap the **+ Button** (in Mom Mode).
2. Choose a source:
   - **Giphy Search:** Find an animated GIF (saves offline automatically).
   - **Symbol Library:** Choose from built-in AAC icons.
   - **Camera/Gallery:** Use your own photos.
3. **Record Audio (Optional):** Tap the Microphone icon to record 2-3 seconds of your voice (e.g., "I want a cookie").
4. Tap **Save**.

*** How to Backup & Restore Data
1. Enter **Mom Mode** (Long press the Lock icon).
2. Tap the **Three Dots Menu** (top right).
3. Choose:
   - **Backup Data:** Creates a ~.zip~ file of all your cards and recordings. You can email it to yourself or save it to Google Drive.
   - **Restore Data:** Select a previously saved backup file to load your cards onto this device.

*** âš ï¸ Critical: Setting up "Kiosk Mode"
To prevent your child from closing the app, you must enable **App Pinning** on your Android device:
1. Go to *Settings > Security > Advanced > App Pinning*.
2. Turn it *ON*.
3. Open Toddler Talk.
4. Swipe up to see recent apps, tap the app icon, and select *Pin*.
