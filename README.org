* Toddler Talk - AAC for Early Intervention

Toddler Talk is an Android (and iOS) application designed specifically for non-verbal toddlers (ages 1-3). Unlike general AAC apps, it prioritizes **instant feedback**, **large motor targets**, and **safety** to prevent accidental edits by curious fingers.

It utilizes evidence-based strategies for early intervention, focusing on specific "Needs" over abstract "Core Words."

** ðŸ“– Parent Guide

*** How to Edit Cards
1. Open the app.
2. Find the **Lock Icon** in the top right corner.
3. **Press and Hold** (Long Press) for 1 second until you see "Mom Mode".
4. You will now see:
  - A "+" button to add new photos.
  - "Delete" icons on custom cards.
  - A "Settings" gear for Backups.

*** How to Backup Data
1. Enter **Mom Mode**.
2. Tap the **Settings Gear**.
3. Select **Backup Data**.
4. Save the ~.zip~ file to your Google Drive or email it to yourself.

*** âš ï¸ Critical: Setting up "Kiosk Mode"
To prevent your child from closing the app, you must enable **App Pinning** on your Android device:
1. Go to *Settings > Security > Advanced > App Pinning*.
2. Turn it *ON*.
3. Open Toddler Talk.
4. Swipe up to see recent apps, tap the app icon, and select *Pin*.
